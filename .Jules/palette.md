## 2025-02-17 - [Accessible Carousel Controls]
**Learning:** Carousel navigation often relies solely on visual icons, leaving screen reader users stuck without knowing what the buttons do.
**Action:** Always add `aria-label` to icon-only buttons (like "Previous image" / "Next image") and ensure image alt text provides context (e.g., "Image 1 of 3").
